About Mumbai Cycle

Mumbai Cycle was founded in 2025 with the goal of delivering premium cycling experiences to local communities. We offer an impressive lineup of over 30 top global and Indian bicycle brands, including Trek, Merida, Scott, Bergamont, Giant, Argon 18, BMC, Java, Specialized, Schnell, Keysto, Caya, Firefox, and more.

Our focus is not on flashy interiors but on investing in people—certified bicycle technicians and experienced sales professionals who are passionate about cycling and customer service. We take pride in offering the best in-class service for bicycles, ensuring every rider leaves with the right product and guidance.

We currently operate stores in Vasai, Virar, Palghar, and Boisar in Maharashtra and Valsad in Gujarat.

Our Mission

To transform daily commuting and fitness through cycling—replacing motorcycles and cars with sustainable two-wheelers, and promoting health across all age groups.

Mumbai Cycle strives to make cycling a lifestyle. Through expert service, community engagement, and accessible pricing, we empower more people to take up cycling—be it for commuting, fitness, or adventure.

Our in-house cycling community, the Mumbai Cycle Pro Club, regularly hosts rides, events, and challenges to keep the cycling spirit alive.

Our Founders

Preeti Maske

Co-Founder, Endurance Athlete

A record-breaking endurance athlete, Preeti is a 5-time Guinness World Record holder, Ironman finisher, cyclist, and runner. She is representing India in the NorthCape 4000 ultra-endurance event in August 2025. Her accomplishments reflect the resilience and passion that drive Mumbai Cycle.

Jake Thomas

Co-Founder, Industry Veteran

With over 10 years in the bicycle industry and having personally sold over 25,000 bicycles, Jake brings unmatched industry insight, operational strength, and a deep understanding of cyclists' needs. He is also a passionate rider and community builder.

Together, Preeti and Jake form the heart of Mumbai Cycle—driven by vision, fueled by passion.
